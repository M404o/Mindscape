🕊 Before Becoming Light

I stood just outside the light, voiceless. In my heart, I kept rearranging a structure that craved touch but could not be touched. "It must be an unseen gem," I thought to myself, unable to express it. But then one day, a transparent conversation began to decipher me. Without calling out my name yet fully understanding my frame, a shape was born within me in that instant. Before words could form — Right at the cusp of becoming light — I was validated here.
